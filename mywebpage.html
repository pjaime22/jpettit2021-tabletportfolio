All About Seals

Welcome! This is a site dedicated to facts about the Atlantic harbor seal and its cousins! Atlantic harbor seals are found in Artic and temperate waters on the eastern coast of North America. They are one of the most prevalent species of seal one can find today! Be sure to explore to find more fun facts about harbor seals and their cousins.
